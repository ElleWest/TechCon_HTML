Frequently Asked Questions

When is TechCon 2024?

TechCon 2024 will be held from March 15-17, 2024, at the Moscone Center in San Francisco, California.

How much does registration cost?

Early bird pricing is $299 for general admission and $149 for students (with valid ID). Regular pricing after December 31, 2023, is $399 and $199 respectively.

What's included with registration?

Registration includes access to all conference sessions, workshops, networking events, lunch and refreshments, conference materials, and a certificate of participation.

Is there a group discount?

Yes! Groups of 5 or more attendees receive a 20% discount on registration fees. Contact our registration team for more information.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We are committed to providing a safe environment for all attendees. Current safety measures include enhanced cleaning protocols, hand sanitizing stations, and optional mask-wearing. We will continue to monitor local health guidelines and update our policies accordingly.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Full refunds are available until February 15, 2024. After that date, a 50% refund is available until March 1, 2024. No refunds are available after March 1, 2024.
