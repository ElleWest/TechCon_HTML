History of TechCon

TechCon was born in 2015 from a simple vision: to create a space where technology professionals could come together to share knowledge, inspire innovation, and build meaningful connections. What started as a small gathering of 200 developers and tech enthusiasts in San Francisco has grown into one of the world's most prestigious technology conferences.

Over the years, TechCon has evolved to reflect the rapid changes in the technology landscape. From the early days of mobile app development to the current era of artificial intelligence and quantum computing, our conference has consistently provided a platform for cutting-edge discussions and forward-thinking presentations.

Today, TechCon attracts over 10,000 attendees annually from more than 50 countries, featuring speakers from leading tech companies including Google, Microsoft, Apple, Amazon, and countless innovative startups that are reshaping our digital world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to democratize access to cutting-edge technology knowledge and foster a global community of innovators, developers, and technology leaders. We believe that technology has the power to solve the world's most pressing challenges, and by bringing together diverse minds and perspectives, we can accelerate progress toward a better future.

Our core values include:

  • Innovation: We champion breakthrough technologies and creative solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible.
  • Inclusivity: We strive to create an environment where everyone, regardless of background or experience level, feels welcome and valued.
  • Education: We are committed to providing high-quality learning experiences that empower attendees to grow their skills and advance their careers.
  • Community: We foster meaningful connections that extend beyond the conference, creating lasting professional relationships and collaborations.

Past Speakers

Dr. Sarah Chen - AI Research Director at Google

Dr. Sarah Chen is a leading researcher in artificial intelligence and machine learning, with over 15 years of experience in developing cutting-edge AI systems. She has published over 100 research papers and holds 25 patents in the field. At TechCon 2023, she delivered a groundbreaking presentation on "The Future of Ethical AI" that has been viewed over 2 million times online.

Marcus Johnson - CTO of TechStart Inc.

Marcus Johnson is a serial entrepreneur and technology visionary who has founded three successful startups, with two achieving billion-dollar valuations. His keynote at TechCon 2022 on "Building Scalable Systems for the Next Decade" provided invaluable insights for thousands of developers and has become a reference point for modern software architecture.

Dr. Priya Patel - Cybersecurity Expert

Dr. Priya Patel is a renowned cybersecurity expert with over 20 years of experience protecting critical infrastructure and enterprise systems. She has advised governments and Fortune 500 companies on security strategies and has been instrumental in developing industry standards for cybersecurity. Her TechCon 2021 presentation on "The Evolving Threat Landscape" helped organizations worldwide strengthen their security postures.

Alex Rodriguez - Open Source Advocate

Alex Rodriguez is a passionate advocate for open source software and has contributed to over 500 open source projects. As the maintainer of several popular JavaScript frameworks, he has helped shape the modern web development landscape. His interactive workshop at TechCon 2020 on "Building the Future with Open Source" inspired hundreds of developers to contribute to open source projects.
